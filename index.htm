Tic Tac Toe

Rules

1. The game is played on a grid of 3 x 3 squares.
2. You are X, your friend (or the computer) is O. Players take turns placing their marks on empty squares.
3. The player who manages to form a line with three identical symbols first, whether horizontally, vertically or diagonally, wins.
4. If at the end of the game all squares are filled and no player has 3 marks in a row, it is a tie.
